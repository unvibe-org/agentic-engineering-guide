### Watch For: Vibe Coding Hangovers

Digging into AI-generated code that "works" but is nonsensical. Stress from not understanding your own codebase. Imposter syndrome from losing your mental model.

The cure: stay close to the code. Review seriously. Never let the AI make architectural decisions without your input. Rebuild your mental model continuously.
